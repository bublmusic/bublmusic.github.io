Revenue Structure

There are three primary revenue streams on Bubl

JukeBox (Pay per play)

Each play of a song using Bubl Credits will net the artist 1p / 1.14 ¢ (UK and USA). This amount is constant.

VIP Passes

Artists will receive £2.50 of the £3.99 (Including VAT) monthly subscription from VIP passes. Bubl receives £0.44p, the remainder is taxes and payment provider costs.

Subscription plays*

Each individual subscription is divided by the total number of monthly plays, giving a 'Per Play Value'. This value is then multiplied by the number of artist plays to give the total monthly revenue. This amount will vary by user.
